About me

Hello, I'm Paulo Henrique, a 21-year-old with a passion for creating things. I really enjoy working with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, sprinkled with a dash of C# expertise. I'm currently honing my skills in React and Angular, because who doesn't love a good challenge? In my spare time, I indulge in the art of crafting stories, masquerading as a hobbyist book writer. Why did the JavaScript developer go broke? Because he kept losing his scripts! But fear not, my coding endeavors are far more successful than my attempts at humor.

If you're seeking a collaborator who can code with a side of wit and bring a refreshing approach to projects, let's connect. I'm open to discussions about job opportunities, interviews, or any tech-related endeavors that could use a touch of creativity and a good laugh. Let's turn the ordinary into extraordinary—because who said coding can't be both efficient and entertaining? Looking forward to our potential collaboration!
